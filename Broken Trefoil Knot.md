## Broken Trefoil Knot
### Description
A [trefoil knot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trefoil_knot), such as [this one](Trefoil%20Knot.md) but with one of the crosses reversed, such that the result is the equivilant of the unknot.
### Notation
$`\{(3,0),(4,1),(2,5)\}`$
### Input
```
3 0
4 1
2 5
end
y

```
